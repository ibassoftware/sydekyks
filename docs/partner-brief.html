Sydekyks · Implementation Partner Brief · Platforms05
What you'd be selling

A shipping roster, and we're only getting started.

The nine Sydekyks below are live and working today, not slideware, a snapshot of the roster as of 18 July 2026. Each connects to the customer's Odoo, takes over a specific job, and reports the exact time and money it saved. They are only the opening lineup: the full roadmap is on the next page.

Finance & Accounts Payable
Ledger
Reads uploaded or emailed supplier invoices and creates draft vendor bills in Odoo (vendor, totals, taxes, line items, original attached). Auto-posts when confident. Kills manual AP data entry.
Mirror
A duplicate-bill watchdog. Catches invoices entered twice, even across vendor records that share a Tax ID or bank account, before they get paid twice.
Shield
An AP fraud-risk detector. Flags bank details changing right before payment, vendor accounts matching employees, and same-person entry-and-approval, then can hold payment for a human auditor.
Sales & CRM
Nudge
Watches open opportunities, flags deals gone quiet, drafts a context-aware follow-up grounded in the last real conversation, and reminds the rep, giving managers a ranked value-at-risk queue.
Quill
An AI proposal & SOW writer. Turns rough notes plus a template into a polished, ready-to-edit document grounded in real Odoo deal facts, refined conversationally and exported to PDF.
Recruiting & HR
Decode
Reads incoming résumés and turns each into a complete, structured applicant record in Odoo, matched to the right role, résumé attached, review flags raised. Zero data entry.
Scout
An AI résumé scorer. Grades each applicant against the job's own requirements and writes a star rating plus a plain-language strengths and weaknesses note: an instant, consistent shortlist.
Documents & Contracts
Seal
Drafts contracts from a template and brief, then runs a clause-by-clause risk review flagging uncapped liability, auto-renewal traps, and weak payment terms. Ships with NDA/MSA/SOW starters.
Signet
An e-signature agent. Emails each signer a secure link, chases stragglers with timed reminders, and files the signed PDF back onto the right Odoo record. Pairs with Seal.
